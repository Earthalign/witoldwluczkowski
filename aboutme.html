About Me

I'm a Software Engineer with several years of commercial experience, primarily focused on the Python and Java ecosystems. My work revolves around building scalable backends, architecting resilient systems, and solving complex engineering problems that require both technical precision and creative thinking.

Beyond pure coding, I take an active interest in the entire software lifecycle. My experience with CI/CD pipelines, monitoring tools like Grafana, and automation servers like Jenkins isn't just about 'DevOps'—it's about ensuring that code is built, tested, and deployed with absolute confidence. Lately, I've been focusing on the human side of engineering: mentoring colleagues, advocating for clean architecture, and helping teams scale not just their software, but their processes as well.
