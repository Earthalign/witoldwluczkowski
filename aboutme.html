About Me

A bit about my journey, the technologies I like, and the values I bring to every engineering team I join.

I am a Python Software Engineer with several years of commercial experience, focused on building clean backend solutions and maintaining high code quality. My daily work is rooted in Agile/Scrum methodologies, where I am used to managing tasks in Jira, documenting solutions in Confluence, and participating in the full development lifecycle—from breaking down requirements to conducting thorough code reviews.

I also bring strong communication skills to the table, shaped by a slightly unconventional background. Before my tech career, I spent 7 years working as a barista in Poland and the UK (great for my English and working under pressure) and over 2 years teaching programming to children. These experiences sparked my passion for public speaking, mentoring, and delivering presentations. I really enjoy sharing knowledge and making complex technical ideas easy for anyone to understand.

Quality & DevOps Delivery

I have a strong background in test automation. I write robust unit tests and manage nightly test suites using Robot Framework. Beyond pure development, I am comfortable working in Linux environments and hands-on with the delivery side of software. While I don’t consider myself a DevOps specialist, I have practical experience building GitLab CI/CD pipelines, configuring Jenkins jobs, and setting up Grafana dashboards to monitor application health. I am continuously expanding my knowledge in this area to bridge the gap between dev and ops.

Tech Passion & AI Workflow

I am driven by a genuine passion for technology, which extends beyond my day job. I regularly build personal side projects to experiment with new tech stacks. My Master’s thesis combined software engineering with Machine Learning, focusing on binary classification and feature selection. Today, I actively leverage modern AI tools (including Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, and tools like Cursor, Whisper Flow, and Antigravity) to optimize my workflow, accelerate prototyping, and automate repetitive tasks.
